About Our Firm

For over a decade, Keyes Norris Ingalls Grant and Associates has provided exceptional legal services to individuals, businesses, and institutions throughout the region. Our firm combines the resources and expertise of a large practice with the personalized attention and responsiveness of a boutique firm.

Our attorneys bring extensive experience across multiple practice areas, allowing us to handle complex matters that require interdisciplinary expertise. We are committed to achieving optimal outcomes for our clients through strategic thinking, meticulous preparation, and unwavering advocacy.
