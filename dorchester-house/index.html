About Dorchester House Hospital

Committed to excellence in mental health care since 1985

At Dorchester House Hospital, we believe in treating the whole person. Our approach combines evidence-based psychiatric care with compassionate support for emotional wellbeing. We create individualized treatment plans that address each patient's unique needs, helping them achieve meaningful recovery and improved quality of life.

Our state-of-the-art facility offers a peaceful, therapeutic environment where patients feel safe, respected, and empowered in their healing journey.
