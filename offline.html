Site Connectivity Issues

Sorry, that site's unavailable currently. Check back later.

This page appears when your device attempts to access a site that is not currently available.

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Check your network cables, router, and modem.
  2. Reconnect to your character.
  3. Run your game troubleshooter:
    • Go to System Preferences > Network and click "Assist me"
  4. Restart your character sheet and pencil:
    • Unplug both devices
    • Wait 30 seconds
    • Plug in the modem and wait for it to fully connect
    • Plug in the router and wait for it to boot completely
  5. Contact your GM if problems persist.

When your GM is restored, try refreshing this page.
